# Architect Recognition

> **Moon Forge is complete and open-source. This document explains the architect's recognition model.**

---

## The 2% Fee (Recognition)

When users exit early and pay penalties, the split is:

```
93.5% → Reward Pool (benefits ALL pilots)
 1.0% → Oracle (gas compensation)
 2.0% → Architect Wallet (recognition)
 2.0% → Referrer (if applicable)
 1.5% → Dev Escrow (returned to pool if growth ≥95%)
```

**What is the 2%?**
- Recognition for creating and open-sourcing the protocol
- Automatic and immutable (hardcoded on-chain)
- Earned only when the protocol is used
- Transparent and verifiable by anyone

**Why does the architect need the 2%?**
The architect doesn't "need" it. It's already complete. The 2% is purely:
- Recognition for the work
- Earned automatically (no begging, no requests)
- Tied to protocol success (architect benefits only if useful)

No development work. No infrastructure costs. No phase 2 funding needed.

## Fair Crypto Philosophy

This model respects fair crypto principles:

1. **No Gatekeeping** — Code is open-source. Fork it. Modify it. Run it locally.
2. **No Mandatory Fees** — Using the protocol costs $0 (except EVM gas).
3. **Transparency** — Every fee is on-chain and verifiable.
4. **Alignment** — Creator benefits only if protocol is successful.
5. **Freedom** — Forks are autonomous. You decide everything.

The architect:
- ✅ Gets automatic recognition (2% from penalties)
- ✅ Can receive donations (completely voluntary)
- ✅ Has no control over forks or users
- ✅ Benefits only if the protocol is useful

## FAQ

**Q: Can the architect remove the 2% fee?**  
A: No. It's hardcoded on-chain. Even the architect can't change it.

**Q: Do I have to keep the architect wallet in my fork?**  
A: No. You own your fork completely. Use your wallet, remove the fee, or keep the original — your choice.

**Q: Is the protocol still being developed?**  
A: No. Moon Forge is complete and ready to use. The architect is not developing Phase 2 or new features. The community can build on top of it.

**Q: What if I want to support the architect?**  
A: You can keep their wallet in your fork (earn 2%) or donate directly (optional). But it's never required.

**Q: Why the 2% then, if the architect isn't developing?**  
A: Recognition. It's a thank you that's automatic and immutable. The architect benefits only if the protocol is useful. Fair and transparent.
