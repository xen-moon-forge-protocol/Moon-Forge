## Fair Launch Philosophy

This project was built as a gift to the XEN and X1 community. There was no funding, no VC, and no prior team allocation.

- **Architect Fee:** The 2% architect fee (hardcoded on-chain) is the only automatic reward for the original creator. This is transparent and immutable.
- **Forks & Modifications:** If you fork this project, you own your version completely. You can keep the architect wallet address, modify it, or remove it — the code is yours.
- **Donations to Pool:** The protocol accepts community donations to the reward vault (100% to pilots). Users never donate directly to the architect.

*May the forge be with you.*

Moon Forge is a decentralized, community-owned protocol. We welcome contributions from anyone who shares our vision of a commitment-based ecosystem for XEN and XNT.

## Core Principles

1. **Anonymity First**: We value privacy and anonymity. Contributions are recognized by code and impact, not by personal identity.
2. **Public Good**: The protocol is designed as a public good. No one "owns" it; the community maintains it.
3. **Decentralization**: We strive for full decentralization in governance, development, and hosting.
4. **Transparency**: All logic is on-chain and verifiable.

## How to Contribute

- **Code**: Fork the repository and submit pull requests for bug fixes, optimizations, or new features.
- **Documentation**: Help improve our guides, whitepaper, and website copy.
- **Tools**: Build analytics, bots, or alternative frontends for the protocol.
- **Community**: Help other users in Discord/Telegram and share the project.

## Development Process

1. **Fork** the repo.
2. **Create a branch** for your feature or fix.
3. **Write tests** for your changes.
4. **Submit a PR** with a clear description of your work.

## Rewards

As an anonymous protocol, there is no central entity to pay for contributions. However, contributors may be recognized by the community or receive grants from any future decentralized treasury.
